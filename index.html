About

Hi, there! I'm Alysha and I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Utah, where I am advised by Dr. Keith Koper. My thesis work focuses on using machine learning to find and process small, often overlapping earthquakes in the Yellowstone volcano region. I'm primarily interested in seismic sources and network seismology and the use of machine learning to help with them.

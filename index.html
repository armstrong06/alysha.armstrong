About

Hi there! I'm Alysha, a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Utah, where Dr. Keith Koper advises me. My thesis focuses on using machine learning to find and process small, often overlapping, earthquakes in the Yellowstone volcano region. I'm primarily interested in seismic sources, network seismology, and machine learning.
